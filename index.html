Our Restaurant

Who we are

The Haukai Restaurant is based in the Bay of Islands, near the Lord of the Rings site. We specialise in Māori kai and welcome all locals and tourists visiting the area to come and experience Te Ao Māori with the use of karakia for blessing food. Our dinner menu features Māori and Pākeha kai options and drinks. View our menu and plan your meal today!

Our owners are here to make your visit as memorable as possible. Experience Māori culture with us! Make a reservation and book your table today.

We are open in the evenings, from Wednesdays to Sundays inclusive. See our hours for more details.

Not sure where we are? Let our map guide you to our special Māori cultured restaurant.
